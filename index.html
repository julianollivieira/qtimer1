All your times are stored locally (on your machine), there is a very big chance that if you clear your browser cookies that all your times are gone.
Make sure you make a backup! (Using the 'Export to file' button in the settings menu)

What's new

×

In update version 1.5
- Both time-reset buttons now clear the timer and all other time indicators.
- Fixed times above 1 minute.
- Big changes under the hood.
- Fixed the chart sometimes not showing or updating.
- Added the mean of three (mo3) indicator.
- Added an indicator to show your best MO3, AO5, AO12 and single times in your current box.


--------------- Older updates ---------------

Changes in version 1.4
- Added the settings menu.
- Added the ability to hide the timer and other components.
- Added a button to clear all boxes to the settings menu.
- Moved the clear current box to the setting menu.
- You can now see your old scrambles (per time) by clicking on a solve in the list.
- Fixed the graph always being on top.
- Fixed box 20.

Changes in version 1.3
- Layout redesigned to fit more screen sizes.
- Added the ability to start/stop the timer using a touchscreen .
- Added the what's new button.
- Added a time graph.

Changes in version 1.2
- Added 20 boxes for more organizable storage.
- Scrambles are now saved per solve.
